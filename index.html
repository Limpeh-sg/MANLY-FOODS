Our Story

Founded in 1986, Manly Pte Ltd is a trusted name in Singapore's food import and distribution industry. We specialize in premium tea, honey, chocolate, and souvenir-friendly FMCG products.

From our early days as a traditional distributor to now being an exclusive brand representative, we have grown to serve retailers, e-commerce platforms, gifting partners, and international visitors across Singapore.
