Our Story of Taste & Tradition

Founded in 1986, Manly Pte Ltd stands as a cornerstone in Singapore's food import and distribution landscape. We pride ourselves on curating premium tea, honey, chocolate, and distinctive souvenir-friendly FMCG products.

Evolving from a traditional distributor to an esteemed exclusive brand representative, Manly now serves a diverse clientele including premier retailers, dynamic e-commerce platforms, corporate gifting partners, and international visitors throughout Singapore.
