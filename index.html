About MANLY

Founded in 1986, MANLY PTE LTD is a trusted name in Singapore's food import and distribution industry. We specialize in premium tea, honey, chocolate, and souvenir-friendly food products, tailored for both tourists and households.

Our commitment to quality, fast response, and curated product selections makes us a reliable partner for B2B clients and gifting customers.

Business Areas

  • Food Distribution to supermarkets & specialty stores
  • Tourist gift shops & travel retail
  • Corporate & Seasonal Gifting (CNY, Hari Raya, Events)
  • Souvenir-focused packaging like Singapore-themed wooden boxes

Our Featured Brands

  • Ahmad Tea - British heritage tea, Halal-certified
  • Mlesna Tea - Premium Ceylon tea in wooden gift boxes
  • Fulmer / Buram Honey - Floral and raw honey for daily use & gifting
  • Merlion Chocolate - In-house brand for tourist gifting
