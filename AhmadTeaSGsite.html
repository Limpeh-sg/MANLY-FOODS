The Art of Brewing: Your Perfect Cup

Crafting the perfect cup of Ahmad Tea is a simple ritual that enhances flavor and aroma. Follow these steps to elevate your tea experience.

1. Water Quality

Always use freshly drawn, filtered water. Avoid re-boiling water as it reduces oxygen content, affecting flavour.

2. Ideal Temperature

Black Teas: 100°C. Green/Herbal Teas: 80°C. Boiling water can burn delicate green leaves, causing bitterness.

3. Steeping Time

Black Teas: 3-5 minutes. Green Teas: 1-3 minutes. Herbal Infusions: 5-7 minutes. Adjust to your desired strength.

4. Serve & Enjoy

Pour and enjoy your perfectly brewed Ahmad Tea. Add milk, sugar, or lemon as preferred. Savour the moment.
